Elaheh Homayounvala PhD

Assistant Professor

Email: e_vala at sbu dot ac dot ir
Google Scholar profile

Elaheh Homayounvala (PhD King’s College London 2006) is assistant professor at Shahid Beheshti University. Before joining SBU, she was research associate at King’s College London, University of London UK and Iranian Research Institute for Information Science and Technology.
She investigates how ICT technology can be adapted to the needs and preferences of users. She is also interested in positive and negative impacts of ICT on our lives.
Her research is multi-disciplinary and includes user modeling and personalisation, adaptive and context-aware user interfaces, management of information technology, technology acceptance, persuasive technologies, augmented reality, m-health and digital identity management systems.
